About Cygor & Quick Guide

Cygor is an automated network reconnaissance and enumeration framework that streamlines the process of discovering and analyzing network services. It combines powerful scanning tools with intelligent automation to help security professionals efficiently assess network infrastructure.

Key Features:
  • Automated Discovery: Leverages Masscan and Naabu for rapid port discovery across large network ranges
  • Intelligent Enumeration: Automatically runs targeted enumeration tools based on discovered services
  • Organized Output: Maintains structured workspace with categorized results for easy analysis
  • Web Dashboard: Real-time monitoring and management of scan tasks and results
Scan Configuration Guide:
  • Top 1000 Ports: Scans the most common ports (faster, recommended for initial discovery)
  • Full Port Scan: Scans all 65535 ports (comprehensive but slower)
  • Masscan: Ultra-fast discovery recommended for large networks (can scan Internet-scale ranges)
  • Naabu: Fast TCP port scanner that can be combined with Masscan for verification
  • Network Interface: Required on multi-homed systems or when scanning from a specific network adapter

Identify your Assets, Targets, Unidentified hosts.

These tools identify live hosts and open ports across your target network. These scanners perform initial reconnaissance to find which systems are online and what ports are accessible.

After discovery completes, Cygor automatically runs detailed Nmap enumeration on discovered hosts to identify services, versions, and gather comprehensive information for further analysis.

Masscan
Ultra-Fast Internet-Scale Scanner
  • Speed: Can scan entire Internet in under 6 minutes
  • Performance: Transmits 10 million packets/second
  • Method: Asynchronous TCP SYN scanning
  • Best For: Large networks (Class B/C ranges)
  • Use Case: Initial discovery on extensive target lists
    • GitHub: robertdavidgraham/masscan
Recommended for networks larger than /24
Naabu
Fast Go-Based Port Scanner
  • Speed: Written in Go for high concurrency
  • Performance: Fast parallel port scanning
  • Method: TCP connect and SYN scanning
  • Best For: Small to medium networks
  • Use Case: Verification scans, focused targets
    • GitHub: projectdiscovery/naabu
Can combine with Masscan for verification
Discovery Workflow
  1. Discovery Phase: Selected tools (Masscan/Naabu) rapidly scan target ranges to identify live hosts and open ports based on your port configuration below
  2. Enumeration Phase: Cygor automatically runs Nmap with service detection (-sV), OS detection, and NSE scripts against discovered hosts
  3. Module Phase: Based on detected services, appropriate enumeration modules (HTTP, SMB, FTP, etc.) run automatically for deeper analysis
